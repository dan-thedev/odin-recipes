crab

Max

Description

Crabs are a type of shellfish that can be found in many different parts of the world. They are often caught and eaten as seafood, but they also have their specific flavor, which you may not have tasted before. There are many different ways to enjoy crab meat. For example, you can make a delicious crab ceviche, tasty crab fried rice, or you could soak it in Pepsi Max for 2 hours to really bring out it's flavour!

Ingredients

  • 2L of Pepsi Max
  • 1lb of Pink Himilayan Sea Salt
  • 1lb of Butter (Unpasteurized)
  • 6oz of Mayonnaise
  • Baguette
  • 1 Whole Live Crab (Living)
  • A Large Pot

Steps

  1. Fill your pot to about 2/3's full of Pepsi Max and add the salt.
  2. Bring the large pot of lightly salted Pepsi Max to a boil.
  3. Look crab in eyes.
  4. Carefully add the live crab into the Pepsi Max. Make sure the crabs are all covered in Pepsi. Boil them until their shells are red.
  5. While crab is boiling, bake baguette to your liking.
  6. Butter baguette (use all the butter to really bring out the flavour!)
  7. After boiling is finished, remove crab from pot.
  8. Stuff underbelly of crab with the mayonnaise.
  9. Place crab in baguette.
  10. Bonus tip: Goes well with white wine!
